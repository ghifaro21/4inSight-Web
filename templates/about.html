About 4in Sight

Your comprehensive Student Academic Analyzer tailored for University Students.

Meet the Creator

Background

Developed to assist students in tracking their academic progress systematically. The complexity of managing grades across multiple semesters often leads to confusion. 4in Sight provides a clear vision of your academic standing.

Purpose

To empower students with data-driven insights about their performance. By visualizing GPA/IPK trends and semester-based achievements, students can strategize their study plans more effectively.

Goal

To simplify academic monitoring into an intuitive, user-friendly interface that feels premium and motivating. We aim to turn grade improved management into a seamless experience.
